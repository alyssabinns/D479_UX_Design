What will you explore?

Start with a few of Taniti’s signature experiences, then customize your trip around what excites you most.

Ocean adventures

Snorkel vibrant reefs, swim with tropical fish, or take a sunset cruise along the coast.

Rainforest & mountain trails

Hike through lush jungle paths, discover hidden waterfalls, and take in sweeping island views.

Island dining

From fresh‑caught seafood to street markets, Taniti’s food scene blends tradition with tropical flavor.

Stay in paradise

Choose from boutique resorts, cozy bungalows, and eco‑friendly lodges surrounded by nature. Whether you want ocean views, rainforest seclusion, or walkable access to town, there’s a stay that fits your style.

Many properties offer guided activities, on‑site dining, and easy transportation so you can focus on enjoying the island.

Getting around Taniti

Taniti is simple and scenic to explore. Most visitors combine walking, shuttles, and bikes to move between beaches, town, and trailheads.

  • Airport arrivals with shuttle connections to major lodging areas
  • Local shuttles running between town, beaches, and key attractions
  • Bike rentals for flexible, car‑free exploring
  • Boat taxis for coastal villages and hidden coves
