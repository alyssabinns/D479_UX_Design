Transportation

Getting around Taniti is simple, scenic, and flexible.

Whether you're arriving by air, exploring beaches, or visiting local villages, Taniti offers convenient and beautiful ways to travel. Choose the option that fits your pace and style.

Airport Arrivals & Shuttles

Taniti’s airport connects you directly to the island’s main town and resort areas. Shuttle services run frequently, offering a smooth and stress‑free way to begin your stay.

  • Frequent shuttles to major lodging areas
  • Friendly staff and easy navigation
  • Perfect for first‑time visitors

Local Bus Routes

Affordable and reliable, Taniti’s bus system connects beaches, markets, and neighborhoods. It’s a great way to explore the island like a local.

  • Budget‑friendly transportation
  • Routes covering key attractions
  • Runs from early morning to evening

Bikes & Boat Taxis

For a more scenic journey, rent a bike to cruise along coastal paths or hop on a boat taxi to reach hidden coves and nearby villages.

  • Bike rentals available across the island
  • Boat taxis for coastal travel
  • Great for adventure‑seekers

Easy, Flexible, and Scenic

No matter how you choose to travel, Taniti’s transportation options make it easy to explore at your own pace. Enjoy the journey as much as the destination.
