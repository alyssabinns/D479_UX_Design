Lodging

Find your perfect stay, from boutique resorts to eco‑friendly bungalows.

Whether you’re dreaming of waking up to ocean views, sleeping beneath rainforest canopies, or staying steps from town, Taniti offers a range of places to call home during your visit.

Beachfront Resort

Stay just steps from the sand with pools, on‑site dining, and easy access to the island’s most popular beach. Perfect for travelers who want comfort, convenience, and amenities.

  • Ocean views and private balconies
  • Resort pools, spas, and restaurants
  • Ideal for couples, families, and groups

Bed and Breakfast

Tucked into lush greenery, these cozy stays offer a quieter, more immersive experience. Listen to birds at sunrise and enjoy cool evenings surrounded by nature.

  • Secluded, nature‑focused settings
  • Smaller properties with local charm
  • Great for travelers seeking calm and privacy

Eco‑Lodges & Budget‑Friendly Inns

Eco‑lodges emphasize sustainability, using local materials and low‑impact practices, while budget‑friendly inns offer simple, comfortable rooms close to town and transit.

No matter your budget or travel style, you’ll find a stay that fits without losing the island’s welcoming atmosphere.
