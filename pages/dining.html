Dining

Taste the island — fresh seafood, tropical fruit, and traditional dishes.

From seaside grills to bustling night markets, Taniti’s dining scene blends fresh ingredients, local traditions, and global flavors. Whether you’re craving a quiet dinner by the water or a lively street‑food adventure, you’ll find something unforgettable on every plate.

Seaside Seafood Restaurants

Enjoy freshly caught fish, grilled shellfish, and bright citrus flavors at open‑air restaurants overlooking the water. Many spots feature daily catch menus and chef specials inspired by traditional island recipes.

  • Oceanfront views and sunset seating
  • Fresh‑caught seafood prepared daily
  • Mix of traditional and modern dishes

Local Markets & Street Food

Wander through colorful markets filled with tropical fruit, baked goods, and savory snacks. Street vendors serve skewers, dumplings, and sweet treats perfect for tasting your way through Taniti’s neighborhoods.

  • Fresh fruit, spices, and local specialties
  • Casual street‑food stalls and night markets
  • Great for sampling many flavors in one visit

Traditional Island Cuisine

Many restaurants and family run eateries offer traditional Tanitian dishes of slow cooked meats, root vegetables, coconut‑based sauces, and shared platters meant for gathering around the table.

Ask about house specialties or seasonal menus to discover flavors that locals love.
